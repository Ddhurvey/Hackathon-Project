Green Salad

120 kcal per serving

Prep Time: 10 min | Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh lettuce
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the lettuce, tomato, and cucumber.
  2. Toss with olive oil and lemon juice in a bowl.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Fruit Smoothie

200 kcal per serving

Prep Time: 5 min | Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries

Instructions

  1. Peel and slice the banana.
  2. Blend banana, berries, almond milk, and honey until smooth.
  3. Serve chilled.

Quinoa Veggie Bowl

180 kcal per serving

Prep Time: 15 min | Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup roasted vegetables
  • 1 tbsp tahini
  • 1 tsp chia seeds

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
  2. Arrange quinoa, avocado, and roasted vegetables in a bowl.
  3. Drizzle with tahini and sprinkle chia seeds on top.

Grilled Salmon

220 kcal per serving

Prep Time: 15 min | Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 1 tsp fresh dill, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Rub salmon with olive oil, lemon zest, dill, and garlic powder.
  2. Grill over medium heat for 4-5 minutes per side.
  3. Serve with a side of steamed vegetables.

Chickpea Salad

150 kcal per serving

Prep Time: 10 min | Servings: 3

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, rinsed
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Instructions

  1. Combine chickpeas, bell pepper, and onion in a bowl.
  2. Mix olive oil and balsamic vinegar for dressing.
  3. Toss salad with dressing and serve chilled.

Veggie Stir Fry

170 kcal per serving

Prep Time: 15 min | Servings: 3

Ingredients

  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add broccoli, bell pepper, and carrot; stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add soy sauce and cook for 2 more minutes.

Greek Yogurt Parfait

140 kcal per serving

Prep Time: 5 min | Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 1/2 cup fresh berries
  • 1 tsp honey

Instructions

  1. Layer Greek yogurt, granola, and berries in a glass.
  2. Drizzle honey over the top.
  3. Serve immediately.
